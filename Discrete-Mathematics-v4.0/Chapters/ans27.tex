\begin{Solution}{27.5}
Luckily we have a base $7$ multiplication table above to make the work a little less onerous. For neatness, the subscript $7$ will be omitted.

\begin{align*}
5122 &= (3)(1312) + 553\\
1312 &= (1)(553) + 426\\
553   &= (1)(426) + 124\\
426   &= (3)(124) + 21\\
124   &= (4)(21) + 10\\
21     &= (2)(10) + 1\\
10     &= (10)(1) + 0\\
\end{align*}

$\gcd(5122_{7}, 1312_{7}) = 1_{7}$.

\end{Solution}
